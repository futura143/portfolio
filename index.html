About Us

IntelliFlow Pte Ltd. is a next-gen technology SaaS platform company that provides tools for companies to design, build and deploy enterprise applications at scale and faster. It innovates and simplifies the application development process through its flagship product. It is a low-code no-code platform and it democratizes access to technology. It allows citizen developers to make anything they can imagine, from enterprise-grade apps that help companies run smoothly to simple tools to save time. IntelliFlow is the low-code platform for everyone to make business better, go live sooner and get to success faster. It provides modern visual interfaces for both citizen and enterprise developers to drag and drop application components, and connect them together to build modern web/ mobile applications. Its powerful features such as form builder, workflow designer, rule engine, data modeller, etc. allow users to design, deploy, manage and track business applications quickly and efficiently.
